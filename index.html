CleanSlate Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 20, 2026

Precision Software ("we," "our," or "us") operates the CleanSlate mobile application ("App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use our App.

By using CleanSlate, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: If you create an account, we collect your email address, display name, and optional profile photo.
  • Recovery Data: Addiction records, quit dates, daily check-in logs (mood, triggers, notes), urge logs, and relapse records that you voluntarily enter.
  • Community Content: Posts, comments, and interactions you make in community support groups.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and app version for compatibility and troubleshooting purposes.
  • Usage Data: Basic app interaction data such as features used and session duration, collected in anonymized and aggregated form only.

1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Precise or approximate location data
  • Contact lists or address books
  • Browsing history outside the App
  • Photos or media (unless you voluntarily upload a profile picture)
  • Financial information (payments are processed by third-party providers)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide the Service: Track your recovery progress, display statistics, deliver milestone notifications, and enable community features.
  • Account Management: Authenticate your identity, sync data across devices, and manage your subscription.
  • Improve the App: Understand usage patterns in aggregate to improve features and fix issues.
  • Communication: Send you milestone achievement notifications and optional daily reminders (which you control).

We do not use your data for advertising, behavioral profiling, or sale to third parties.

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

CleanSlate is designed as an offline-first app. If you use the App without creating an account, all your data is stored locally on your device only. We cannot access locally-stored data.

3.2 Cloud Storage

If you create an account, your data is stored securely using Supabase (hosted on AWS infrastructure) with the following protections:

  • Row-Level Security (RLS) policies ensuring users can only access their own data
  • Encryption in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest
  • Secure authentication via email/password, Google OAuth, or Apple Sign-In

3.3 Data Isolation

Your recovery data is private by default. Only community posts you voluntarily publish are visible to other users. Your addiction records, daily logs, urge logs, and progress statistics are never shared with other users.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Cloud database, authentication, and real-time features. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat: Subscription and payment management. RevenueCat processes purchase receipts but does not have access to your recovery data. RevenueCat Privacy Policy
  • Google Sign-In / Apple Sign-In: Optional authentication providers. We receive only your email address and display name from these services.

We do not use advertising SDKs, analytics trackers, or data brokers.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to anyone. We may share data only in these limited circumstances:

  • With your consent: Community posts you choose to publish are visible to other group members.
  • Service providers: Our infrastructure providers (Supabase, RevenueCat) process data as described above, solely to provide their services.
  • Legal requirements: If required by law, subpoena, or legal process, though we will attempt to notify you where possible.

6. Your Rights and Choices

  • Use without an account: You can use CleanSlate entirely offline without providing any personal information.
  • Access your data: All your data is visible to you within the App at all times.
  • Delete your data: You can delete your account and all associated data at any time through Settings > Delete Account. This action is permanent and cannot be undone.
  • Control notifications: You can enable or disable all notifications and reminders in the App settings.
  • Withdraw consent: You can stop using the App at any time. Uninstalling removes all local data.

7. Data Retention

  • Active accounts: We retain your data for as long as your account is active.
  • Deleted accounts: When you delete your account, all personal data is permanently removed from our servers immediately.
  • Local data: Remains on your device until you uninstall the App or clear app data.

8. Children's Privacy

CleanSlate is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete that information promptly. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

9. Community Content Moderation

Community posts may be reviewed for compliance with our community guidelines. Posts that receive multiple reports from users may be automatically flagged for review. We reserve the right to remove content that violates our guidelines or applicable law.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy and, where appropriate, through an in-app notification. Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. California Residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect and how it is used
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell your data)
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

12. European Residents (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have rights under the GDPR including:

  • Right of access to your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restriction of processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

Our legal basis for processing is your consent (which you can withdraw at any time by deleting your account) and legitimate interests in providing and improving the service.
