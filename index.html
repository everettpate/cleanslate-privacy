1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Voluntarily

  • Account Information: If you choose to create an account, we collect your email address, display name, and optional profile photo. Account creation is optional — the App can be used without an account.
  • Recovery Data: Addiction records, quit dates, daily check-in logs (mood rating, triggers, notes, pledges), urge logs (intensity, triggers, outcome), and relapse records that you voluntarily enter into the App.
  • Community Content: Posts, comments, likes, and interactions you make in community support groups. Community participation is optional and requires an account.
  • Payment Information: If you subscribe to CleanSlate Pro, payment processing is handled entirely by Google Play, Apple App Store, and/or RevenueCat. We do not collect, store, or have access to your credit card numbers, bank account details, or other financial information.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and app version for compatibility and troubleshooting purposes.
  • Usage Data: Basic app interaction data such as features used and session duration, collected in anonymized and aggregated form only. This data cannot be used to identify individual users.
  • Crash Data: In the event of an app crash, anonymized diagnostic information may be collected to help us fix bugs and improve stability.

1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Precise GPS location data (note: IP addresses used by our infrastructure providers may indicate general geographic region, but we do not use this to track or profile you)
  • Contact lists or address books
  • Browsing history outside the App
  • Photos or media from your device (unless you voluntarily upload a profile picture)
  • Credit card numbers or financial account details
  • Biometric data (fingerprints, face scans)
  • Call logs or SMS messages
  • Data from other apps on your device

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide the Service: Track your recovery progress, display statistics and streaks, deliver milestone notifications, and enable community support features.
  • Account Management: Authenticate your identity, sync data across your devices, and manage your subscription status.
  • Improve the App: Understand usage patterns in aggregate to improve features, fix bugs, and enhance user experience. We do not build individual user profiles for this purpose.
  • Communication: Send you milestone achievement notifications and optional daily reminders that you configure and control. We do not send marketing emails or promotional notifications.
  • Safety and Moderation: Monitor community content for violations of community guidelines, including content that may be harmful, abusive, or inappropriate.

We do not use your data for advertising, behavioral profiling, targeted marketing, or sale to third parties. We do not use your health or recovery data for any purpose other than providing the App's core functionality to you.

3. Sensitive Data Handling

We recognize that recovery and addiction data is deeply personal and sensitive. We treat all recovery-related data with the highest level of care:

  • Your recovery data is never shared with other users, advertisers, data brokers, employers, insurance companies, or any other third party.
  • Your recovery data is never used for profiling, scoring, or any form of automated decision-making.
  • Community posts are the only user-generated content visible to other users, and only within the groups where they are posted.
  • We do not infer or derive additional health information from your data beyond what you explicitly enter.

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage (Offline Mode)

CleanSlate is designed as an offline-first app. If you use the App without creating an account, all your data is stored exclusively on your device. We have no access to, and cannot retrieve, locally-stored data. If you uninstall the App or clear app data, locally-stored data is permanently deleted.

4.2 Cloud Storage (With Account)

If you create an account, your data is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database platform hosted on Amazon Web Services (AWS) infrastructure, with the following protections:

  • Row-Level Security (RLS): Database policies ensure that each user can only access their own data. Even in the event of an application-level vulnerability, database-level security prevents unauthorized cross-user data access.
  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmitted between the App and our servers is encrypted using TLS 1.2 or higher.
  • Encryption at Rest: All stored data is encrypted at rest using industry-standard AES-256 encryption.
  • Secure Authentication: We support email/password authentication with secure hashing, Google OAuth 2.0, and Apple Sign-In.
  • Access Controls: Administrative access to databases and servers is restricted to authorized personnel only, with audit logging enabled.

4.3 Data Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will:

  • Notify affected users via email without unreasonable delay and as required by applicable law
  • Describe the nature of the breach and the types of data involved
  • Describe the measures we are taking to address the breach and mitigate potential harm
  • Notify relevant regulatory authorities as required by applicable law

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services, each of which has its own privacy policy governing their use of data:

  • Supabase: Cloud database, authentication, and real-time features. Supabase processes your data on our behalf as a data processor. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat: Subscription and payment management. RevenueCat processes purchase receipts and subscription status only. It does not have access to your recovery data, community posts, or any health-related information. RevenueCat Privacy Policy

We do not use advertising SDKs, analytics trackers, social media pixels, data brokers, or any third-party tools designed to track, profile, or retarget users.

We do not sell, trade, rent, or monetize your personal information in any way. We may disclose data only in these strictly limited circumstances:

  • Legal compliance: If required by law, valid subpoena, court order, or governmental regulation. We will attempt to notify you in advance where legally permitted.

7. Your Rights and Choices

  • Use without an account: You can use CleanSlate entirely offline without providing any personal information whatsoever.
